The Host: Julie Chen Moonves
Julie Chen Moonves, husband of former CBS director Les Moonves, has been the host of Big Brother ever since it premiered in July 2000. Chen Moonves has been hosting an American television longer than anyone in the nation, as well as being a spokesperson and moderator on the show "The Talk". As the host, she gives immediate interviews to evicted housguests and reads out to the houseguests who has been evicted all the way up until the finale.
