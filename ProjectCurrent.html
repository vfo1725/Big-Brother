THE GAME IS ON!
 
The 2019 game of Big Brother is underway until September 12, and this year is all about camping! The house is set to an outdoor theme and the houseguests have an unexpected season coming at them. This season, there has already been some fights, alliances forming, and huge twists that are affecting the game like never before. The season has only just kicked off, so now is the time to catch up on the how the game is going. Shows air every Sunday, Wednesday, and Thursday. See below for more on the season! Camp Comeback (Members):

Camp comback is a new twist this season and acts as a variation of the Battle Back competition. The first four evicted houseguests, for the first time in history, would not be leaving the Big Brother house, but instead stay in the house as members of Camp comback. As a member of camp comeback, the evicted housguests would be Have-Nots and not particpiate in any other competitions in the game. After the first four members of camp comeback are there, all three of them would compete in one competition for one houseguest to return to the game, and the other three will go home for good.
  • Members of Camp Comeback:
    • Kemi
    • David
    • Cliff
    • Ovi
Whacktivity Competition:

Another twist this season is the Whacktivity Competition. These "scary" competitions allows 5 houseguests to play for a secret power, this being held in complete secret if the houseguests wish. The houseguests sign up for one of three Whacktivity competition: The Panic Comp, The Chaos Comp, The Nightmare Comp: Below are the winners of the Whacktivity competitions that have happened:
  • Ovi - Nightmare Power: He can anonymously change the nominations at the nomination cermony himself within the next six weeks.
  • Jack - Chaos Power: He can redraw a new group of veto players if he does not like the veto drawing at any of the next eight veto pickings.
  • Christie - Panic Power:  She won the Diamond Power of Veto, which can upgrade the veto holder's power to not only pull someone off the block, but also choose a replacement nominee instead of the HOH.
