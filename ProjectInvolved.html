HOW TO SIGN UP TO PLAY:

Ever wanted to see how you would do at the Big Brother Game? Below is how you can possibly sign up to get a chance to be in the game. See below, if you are a United States citizen, if you are old enough to be considered to play!

Enter Age Here: (TRY IT!)   
Result:

If you matched the age, you still must meet the requirements below:
  • You must be willing to travel to Los Angeles on your own accord and expenses to participate in an interview to be selected on the show, this being if you make it past the first round of cuts.
  • You must be willing to be in the Big Brother House for approximately 100 days away from the interent and any work you are involved in.
  • You must be in perfect or excellent mental and physical health condititons
If you meet all of the above requirements, your first step in applying will be to fill out an online application AND submit a video of yourself expressing why you should be considered to be on the show. Visit the casting website for more information below.
